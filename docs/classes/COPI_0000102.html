Natural Language DefinitionDefinição em Linguagem Natural
EN
A centrifugal pump is a pump that uses centrifugal force generated by rotating impellers with vanes to increase fluid pressure through a volute casing.

3 Differentia SpecificaDiferença Específica
∃hasComponentPartAtAllTimes.CentrifugalImpeller ⊓ ∃hasComponentPartAtAllTimes.VoluteCasing

Distinguished from other pumps by having centrifugal impellers with vanes that generate centrifugal force and a volute casing that converts this force to pressure.

The centrifugal impeller is the key component that creates centrifugal force, and the volute casing converts this kinetic energy to pressure. These are the minimal structural elements that distinguish centrifugal pumps from all other pump types.
